Place Quaggan

For Web Devs Who Love Guild Wars 2

Cooooo, Quaggan likes filler images!

Add the attribute src="placequaggan.co.uk/quaggan?width=400&height=400" to your image tag for a placeholder image of an adorable Quaggan!

For Example:

placequaggan.co.uk/quaggan?width=400&height=400

placequaggan.co.uk/quaggan?width=800&height=600

placequaggan.co.uk/quaggan?width=100&height=100
